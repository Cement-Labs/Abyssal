## 使用手册

<div align="center">
  <img width="700" src="/Docs/Contents/简体中文/Overview.png?raw=true" />
</div>

### 基础知识

**Abyssal**将你的菜单栏分成三个区域 - **永远隐藏区域、隐藏区域**以及**显示区域：**

- **永远隐藏区域**&emsp;在此区域内的图标将会被<i>永远隐藏，</i>除非你手动查看它们。
- **隐藏区域**&emsp;在此区域内的图标遵循某些显示规则。大多数时候，*你将不会见到它们。*
- **显示区域**&emsp;在此区域内的图标将会正常显示，*不受限制。*


这三个区域被两个分隔符切分 - `永远隐藏分隔符`&ensp;<sub><picture><source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Dark/DottedLine.png?raw=true" /><img height="17" src="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Light/DottedLine.png?raw=true" /></picture></sub>（位于离屏幕角落最远的一侧）和`隐藏分隔符`&ensp;<sub><picture><source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Dark/Line.png?raw=true" /><img height="17" src="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Light/Line.png?raw=true" /></picture></sub>（位于中间）。Apart from this, there's another separator on the right - the `菜单分隔符`&ensp;<sub><picture><source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Dark/Dot.png?raw=true" /><img height="17" src="/Docs/Contents/Icons/Light/Dot.png?raw=true" /></picture></sub> (the leading one), its position doesn't matter, but it plays an important role.

> **Abyssal** will judge the order of the three separators, which means you don't need to care much about their position. For example, you are allowed to put the `菜单分隔符` to the left of the `永远隐藏分隔符`, as they will swap their roles back automatically after your operation.

<br />

### 显示和移动分隔符

In themes including the default theme, separators will be hidden by default. And when you open the menu, or move your cursor onto the menu bar[^cursor_onto_status_bar] and press the chosen modifiers, **Abyssal** will show all the separators. In the rest of the themes, separators won't be hidden, but their appearance will change automatically according to the status of the app. The show and hide of the separators also follow some special rules:

- When using themes that automatically hide the icons inside the separators, the `菜单分隔符` will indicate the visibility of the status icons inside the **隐藏区域**. If the `菜单分隔符` **is visible,** it indicates that the status icons inside the **隐藏区域** are **visible.** Otherwise the icons are **hidden.**
- When using other themes, all the separators perform together. If all of them are **translucent,** it indicates that the status icons inside the **隐藏区域** are **visible.** Otherwise the icons are **hidden.**

[^cursor_onto_status_bar]: You need to move your cursor to the left of `菜单分隔符` in order to trigger something. On monitors with notches, you may also need to move your cursor *between the the screen notch and the `菜单分隔符`.*

Dragging the icons while holding <kbd>⌘ command</kbd> can change the order of the separators. For example, to put more icons into or out of the **隐藏区域.**

<br />

### 点按分隔符

You can perform different actions by clicking on the separators of **Abyssal,** no matter whether they are visible:

<br />

#### 永远隐藏区域分隔符

- **<kbd>单击</kbd>&emsp;/&emsp;<kbd>右键单击</kbd>**
  
  **Show / hide** the status icons inside the **隐藏区域.**

<br />

#### 隐藏区域分隔符

- **<kbd>单击</kbd>&emsp;/&emsp;<kbd>右键单击</kbd>**
  
  **Show / hide** the status icons inside the **隐藏区域.**

<br />

#### 菜单分隔符

- **<kbd>单击</kbd>**
  
  **Show / hide** the status icons inside the **隐藏区域.**

- **<kbd>⌥ option</kbd> <kbd>单击</kbd>**
  
  **Open / close** the preferences menu.

<br />

## 自定义

Let's take a look at how to customize **Abyssal**. Still remember? You can open the preferences menu by <kbd>右键单击</kbd>&ensp;/&ensp;<kbd>⌥ option</kbd> <kbd>单击</kbd> on the `菜单分隔符`.

The preferences are explained as follows:

<br />

### 主题

`样式：弹出式菜单`
  
Separators' appearances differ from theme to theme, and some themes will automatically 隐藏分隔符s, while others will not.

<br />

### 自动显示

`样式：开关`&ensp;`默认：开`
  
- **开**
    
  **Abyssal** will hide the status icons inside the **隐藏区域** automatically when you are not using them, and show them when you need.
    
- **关**
    
  You will need to control the visibility of the status icons manually.

<br />

### 反馈强度

`样式：步进滑动条`&ensp;`默认：● · · · 禁用`

- **`● · · ·`&emsp;禁用**
  
  Haptic feedback **Disabled.**
  
- **`· ● · ·`&emsp;轻**
  
  **Light** haptic feedback intensity.
  
- **`· · ● ·`&emsp;中**
  
  **Medium** haptic feedback intensity.
  
- **`· · · ●`&emsp;重**
  
  **Heavy** haptic feedback intensity.

> [!NOTE]
> Due to the limitations of macOS, you may not feel a strong haptic feedback.

<br />

### 盲区宽度

`样式：滑动条`&ensp;`默认：⋯⋯●⋯⋯⋯⋯⋯⋯ 25%`

This option appears only on screen without notches and configures the ignored status bar width on these screens in percentage.

Due to the limitations of macOS, **Abyssal** can't infer the available width used for collapsing. You may need to toggle it manually in order to acquire a better experience.

<br />

### 使用永远隐藏区域

`样式：开关`&emsp;`默认：开`

- **开**
  
  **Completely disable** the **永远隐藏区域** and **hide** the `永远隐藏分隔符`.

- **关**

  **Enable** the **永远隐藏区域** and **show** the `永远隐藏分隔符`.

<br />

### 修饰键

`样式：开关列表`&emsp;`默认：⌥ ⌘`

- **`⌃`**

  Whether to use <kbd>⌃ control</kbd> as a modifier key.

- **`⌥`**

  Whether to use <kbd>⌥ option</kbd> as a modifier key.

- **`⌘`**

  Whether to use <kbd>⌘ command</kbd> as a modifier key.

> [!NOTE]
> Pressing only one of the chosen keys is enough to trigger the functions. It is recommended to keep the modifier key <kbd>⌘ command</kbd> enabled.

<br />

### 超时

`样式：步进滑动条`&emsp;`默认：· · · ● · · · · · · · 30秒`

Set an optional timeout for `Auto Idling` to cancel automatically, ranging from `5 seconds` to `10 minutes`.

- **`· · · · · · · · · · ●`&emsp;禁用**

  **Disable** timeout for `Auto Idling`.

<br />

### 减少动画

`样式：开关`&emsp;`默认：关`

- **开**

  Animate the moves of **Abyssal.**

- **关**

  Reduce animations to gain a more performant experience.

<br />

> [!WARNING]
> If you are using an **Intel** chip Mac, it is recommended to keep the `Reduce Animation` option **开** to ensure the smoothness of **Abyssal.**

<br />

### 随 macOS 启动

`样式：开关`&emsp;`默认：关`

- **关**

  **Abyssal** will run in background after macOS launched.

- **开**

  You need to manually run **Abyssal** after macOS launched.

<br />

### 附加功能：自动闲置

Due to the limitations of macOS, **Abyssal** cannot know whether you have opened a menu in the **永远隐藏区域** or the **隐藏区域.** If the **Auto Hide** function hides these status icons rashly, their menus will also move away. Therefore, **Abyssal** adopts an approach to avoid similar situations to the greatest extent.

Speaking generally, when you click on a place in the menu bar **where there is likely to have other status icons, and the status icon is likely to be inside the 隐藏区域 or the 永远隐藏区域,** **Abyssal** will choose to pause the **Auto Hide** and enter the **Auto Idling** state. When you finish the operation, just move the cursor **over** the `永远隐藏分隔符` or the `隐藏分隔符`, and you can cancel the **Auto Idling** state and resume **Auto Hide** to hide the status icons. **Abyssal** also provides an optional timeout to automatically disable the **Auto Idling** state, which can be configured in the preferences menu.

**Auto Idling** will enable automatically accordng to your clicking position, and it will distinguish between the **永远隐藏区域** and the **隐藏区域.** It will only be activated when **Auto Hide** is enabled.

After you **triggered or canceled** **Auto Hide or Auto Idling,** **Abyssal** will give a haptic feedback[^haptic_feedback_support_needed].

[^haptic_feedback_support_needed]: 你的设备需要支持*Haptic 触感反馈。*

## 安装和运行

>[!NOTE]
> As an open-source and free software, **Abyssal** can't afford an [Apple Developer Account.](https://developer.apple.com/help/account/) Therefore, you can't install **Abyssal** directly from App Store, and you may need to allow **Abyssal** to run as an unidentified app[^open_as_unidentified].
